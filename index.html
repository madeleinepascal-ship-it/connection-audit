So what does this all mean?
The ad industry is full of good people? Or maybe just that we're a bunch of shameless networkers. Or, social media has lemming'd us into adding everyone we can think of whenever a new platform takes hold? Probably all three.

Everyone should go to more weddings, conferences, and join more leagues and clubs. Duh, this is nothing new. In-person interaction is easier when there's something shared that you're all there to take part in. Gives you something to discuss, commiserate about, etc.

Also, job interviews can yield some new friends — even when you don't get the job. Fun fact: most of the people I'm connected to that I met in a job interview didn't hire me. But a few of them are friends for life.
